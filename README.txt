WARNING:
There are sounds! ***ALSO*** this game accesses your default microphone device.
This is necessary to progress through the game.

CONTROLS:
E to interact (pickup items, give items/speak to bird)
Space to Continue dialogue

-When standing in circle of pink rocks, your default audio input device will be activated.
You must make a loud enough sound (comfortable speaking volume, at least. Singing would be ideal
if only to fit the themes of the narrative) in order to effect the gameworld (make a pathway appear to you).
